An overview of our project:

A boba shop website where users can browse pictures of the boba drinks that our shop has to offer and add items to their cart. For a specific item/boba drink, there is a dedicated page for it where users can submit reviews of that drink, see the reviews that other users submitted for that drink, and also the nutritional info for that drink. In addition, we also have functionality where authenticated users can add to their cart. Any authenticated user can submit a review(we do not plan to have functionality that processes payments so we would not be able to differentiate whether a user purchased this drink or not). A user with an account will be able to set their profile picture for their account and all the reviews they submitted(while logged in) will be displayed on their details page
